Information About The Toll Plaza Simulation

The simulation is 24 minutes long, where each minute is an hour and a second is a minute in the simulation.
There are 4 types of vehicles:

  • Car
  • Truck
  • Motorcycle
  • Bus
The simulation updates the number of vehicles and the type of vehicles every second in real time, which is a mminute in the simulation.
Additionally, the number of each type of vehicles, the number of total vehicles a minute, and the type of each vehicle varies by simulation time.
  • 12am - 5am: 30% Cars, 65% Trucks, 5% Motorcycles, 0% Buses (2 - 10 vehicles per minute)
  • 5:01am - 9am: 50% Cars, 25% Trucks, 5% Motorcycles, 20% Buses (20 - 40 vehicles per minute)
  • 9:01am - 2pm: 40% Cars, 40% Trucks, 10% Motorcycles, 10% Buses (5 - 20 vehicles per minute)
  • 2:01pm - 4pm: 45% Cars, 30% Trucks, 10% Motorcycles, 15% Buses (10 - 30 vehicles per minute)
  • 4:01pm - 6pm: 60% Cars, 15% Trucks, 5% Motorcycles, 20% Buses (20 - 40 vehicles per minute)
  • 6:01pm - 8pm: 60% Cars, 25% Trucks, 5% Motorcycles, 10% Buses (10 - 30 vehicles per minute)
  • 8:01pm - 11:59pm: 50% Cars, 40% Trucks, 5% Motorcycles, 50% Buses (5 - 15 vehicles per minute)
Next, the value of the toll, chance of having an EZ-Pass or being residential depends on each type of vehicle.
If a vehicle does not have an EZ-Pass, then it is automatically not a residential vehicle.
  • A car or a motorcycle will have an EZ-Pass 70% of the time and gets a residential discount 60% of the time. The toll is $12.
  • A Truck will have an EZ-Pass 90% of the time. The toll is $18.
  • A bus will always have an EZ-Pass. The toll is $0.
Lastly, the summary table breakdown:
  • How many of each type of vehicle passed through the plaza
  • How many of each type of vehicle had an EZ-Pass
  • How many of each type of vehicle was residential
  • The total money collected via EZ-Pass
  • The total money collected via pay by plate
  • The percentages of all the totals
